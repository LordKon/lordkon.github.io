Welcome to CCAS Certification

The Certified Cryptoasset Anti-Financial Crime Specialist (CCAS) certification equips professionals with the knowledge to combat financial crime in the cryptocurrency space. Learn how to identify, mitigate, and manage risks related to money laundering and other illicit activities in the crypto industry.

Course Details

Our comprehensive program covers the essentials of AML compliance in the crypto space, including:

  • Introduction to cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology
  • Understanding financial crime typologies and risks in cryptoassets
  • Techniques for tracing transactions on the blockchain
  • Best practices for due diligence and risk management
  • Global AML regulations, including the FATF Travel Rule

The course is delivered through self-paced online modules, with real-world case studies to enhance your learning experience.

Benefits of CCAS Certification

  • Gain a globally recognized certification in AML for the crypto sector
  • Enhance your career in compliance, law enforcement, or financial services
  • Demonstrate expertise in managing crypto-related financial crime risks
